Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

Overview

PayPal Currency Converter ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App operates entirely on your device. We do not:

  • Require account registration
  • Collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data

Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your last used currency pair
  • Your favorite currency pairs

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Exchange Rate Data

The App fetches real-time exchange rates from Yahoo Finance API. No personal data is transmitted in these requests.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
